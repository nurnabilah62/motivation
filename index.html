Motivation for Online Education

Online learning is education that takes place over the Internet.
It is often referred to as “elearning” among other terms.
It is the type of learning takes place across distance and not in a traditional classroom.
Online education is one of the most dynamic and enriching forms of
learning that exist today. Online education is a subcategory of distance education,
which has been defined as the formal delivery of instruction in which time and
geographic location separate students and instructors.

What motivates me?

Every student can learn, just not on the same day or in the same way

  • By doing online education, it teaches me on how to be an independent person.
    Since online learning required individual assignments for all the subject for this
    semester, so I need to complete the tasks given by lecturers on my own.
  • It teaches me about time management and I have the flexibility to create my own
    schedules during at home. Through online learning, I need to be more focus and alert
    about the task and assignments. This is because I need to allocate time well in completing
    the tasks.
  • It can demonstrated self-motivation in which I can tackle multiple tasks, set priorities,
    and adapt to changing work conditions.
  • Online learning can enhance my technical skills in using computer. I will be more familiar
    with new tools and software. Other than that, most of the assessments need to be made
    by using video presentation. So, I need to learn how to edit the video. Indirectly, it will
    increased my knowledges about information technology (IT).
  • When learning through online, I can revisit past material or stop the lecture to perform
    additional research or organize my notes. I can work through the lesson plan at my own
    pace to ensure I am really mastering the chapter before proceed to the next section.
